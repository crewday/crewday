Your home organization operating system

From a single house to 50 hotels. From a single part time maid to 500.

crew.day is the operations back-office for owners and small property-management outfits — designed so an LLM agent runs the day-to-day and the humans live in the house.

I
Ask anywhere

A vast back-office. The easiest to use.

Every schedule, payslip, warranty and bedsheet lives inside crew.day. On top of it — a concierge who already knows how to use the whole system, waiting for your word.

Reach them wherever you happen to be: the browser at your desk, the dedicated app in your pocket, a WhatsApp thread mid-errand. Same memory, same house, same conversation. You just ask; the agent does.

Fig. 2 — one bell, three doors
II
Places · People · Tasks

A ledger for who does what, where.

Properties, staff, stays, tasks, payroll — all modelled around the ways a small outfit actually works. Owners and managers see everything; workers see today; clients see the slice that is theirs. One data model, three audiences, no double-entry.

Turnovers auto-generate from bookings. Evidence photos close tasks. Hours roll up to payslips. The boring parts stay boring.

Fig. 3 — ledger
III
Assets · Actions · Inventory

Inventory and assets, on autopilot.

Every appliance, boiler and pool pump lives on the books — with its warranty, its manual, its next service due. When does the AC warranty expire? How do I change the dryer filter? Ask the agent; the answer is already there.

Inventory moves with the work. Each turnover draws down the linens; each service burns its filter. You know how many clean sheets are in the cupboard — and when to reorder, before the last one's gone.
